Import leads

In Meta: Ads Manager (or Leads Center) → your lead form → Download leads (CSV), then import it here. Duplicate phone numbers are skipped automatically, so you can re-import the full file every few days.

Backup & move between devices

⚠️ Leads are stored in this browser only (nothing is uploaded anywhere). To use both phone and laptop: export a backup on one device and restore it on the other — restore merges, keeping whichever copy of each lead is most recently updated, so you won't lose anything.

WhatsApp message templates
